# AI CO-HOST — Architecture Overview

## Project Vision

AI Co-Host is an AI-powered hospitality platform designed for:
- Airbnb hosts
- Property managers
- Short-term rental operators

The platform centralizes:
- guest communication
- AI concierge
- welcome books
- check-in flows
- cleaning operations
- billing/subscriptions
- operational management
